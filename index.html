Our Story

Rooted in the Heart of Eluru

Eluru Cafe was born from a simple belief — that the most honest food comes from the most honest ingredients. Our founders, native to the vibrant town of Eluru in Andhra Pradesh, carried recipes passed down through four generations to bring you tiffins that taste exactly like what grandmother made.

We source our rice from the Cauvery delta, our lentils from local farmers, and our coconuts fresh each morning. Our idli batter ferments overnight, our chutneys are ground by stone, and our sambar simmers for hours. No shortcuts. Ever.
